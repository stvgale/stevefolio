Competitive Analysis

We started with a brand discovery to gain a deep understanding of the construction industry, and help the team consider their voice and tone. Through those insights we determined who their target customer was, and how to best approach them through Ikka's brand materials and website.

Brand

The brand needed to be welcoming and trustworthy, but also communicate Ikka's progressive approach to construction. We decided on a muted color pallette so Ikka's work could become the focal point. The simplified logo is easy to read across multiple mediums, and will evolve with the times.

Website design

The new website is clean, responsive and focuses on project progress to solution. We focused on large imagery and clear typography to tell their story.

Learnings/takeaways

  • Users will research and find things on their own.
  • Message is important and needs to be authentic.
