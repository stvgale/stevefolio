Challenge

Create a Design System that provides a cohesive user experience, accelerate our design process and help to bring our product to life faster.

PROCESS

  1. 1. Research

  2. 2. Design

  3. 3. Test

Research

Deconstructed the products into users, user flows, processes, sitemaps and individual components. Listened to sales pitches,user interviews and product demos to gain a better understanding of user needs and business goals.

Workshop

My team and I traveled to Tampa to meet with stakeholders to review user testing, interviews and workshopped ideas for solutions. Created storyboards, solutions sketch, workflows and interactions.

Wireframe

Created wireframes and prototypes of each workflow scenario with multiple user types

Test

Designs were prototyped and demoed during an event with real users of the product to collect feedback in person:

KEY TAKEAWAYS

  • Lots of enthusiasm for new design
  • The new layout is intuitive for people used to the system, and for new users who are familiar with Microsoft (almost all)
  • Want personalization and interactivity for dashboards
  • Very useful feedback on granular UI details
  • Good contacts for additional testing and research - customers are excited about being part of the process, once they understand the value of UX



Follow up interviews and demos were done throughout the design and implementation process with some of the users.

UI

Worked with UI designers and developers to see final product through.
